Approval Process

When your game is complete, you will probably want to publish it for all the world to see. If you plan to distribute your game through Google's Android Market, make sure that you are prepared to sign up and agree to their terms. Portions of your game that rely on OpenFeint servers for free hosting must meet OpenFeint's terms and pass approval. This process is initiated by the "Prepare for Submission" button on the OpenFeint developer dashboard. Select Android as the platform to be approved, accept the terms and conditions, and hit the submit button.
